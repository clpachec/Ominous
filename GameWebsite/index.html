Charles

Discover

Charles is a game about solving puzzles and defeating monsters. It will take patience and critical thinking to solve each puzzle hidden within the house. Through secret messages, hidden artifacts, and an interactive environment, the player should derive a keen sense of what to do. Search and explore the house for as many clues as you can find!

Companion

To fully explore the shadow infested house and solve the challenging puzzles ahead, all the while staying alive, you will need all the help you can get. The dog in the house will be your eyes and ears when you are completely alone and lost. He will be a companion you can confide in when all hope is lost.

Health

Lurking deep within the house are shadow monsters that will inflict severe damage upon physical contact with Charles. Be cautious as you explore rooms and corridors throughout the house. Solving puzzles will only enrage the shadow-beings, so keep an eye on your health as you explore!

Flashlight

Throughout the game Charles will discover many useful items, but none more so than his flashlight. The flashlight is Charles main weapon of defense and you will need it if you wish to leave this house alive.
